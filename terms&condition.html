Overview

The SME Store offers SME members and nonmembers access to an extensive collection of manufacturing content including:

  • Books, DVDs, and CD-ROMS
  • eBooks and eChapters
  • Streaming video on-demand and video download
  • Technical papers
  • Journals and yearbooks

Ordering

  • Online: Use your American Express, MasterCard, Visa, or Discover card to order online at sme.org/store.
  • Phone: Call SME Customer Care between 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. ET, Monday-Friday at 800.733.4763 or 313.425.3000 ext. 4500.
  • Mail: Send payment to SME, Customer Care Department, PO Box 6028, Dearborn, MI 48121.

Payment

All orders must be accompanied by money order, check, purchase order (subject to credit approval), or valid credit card information (American Express, MasterCard, Visa, and Discover). Remit payment in U.S. funds drawn on U.S. banks. All prices subject to change without notice.

Volume Discounts

A volume discount is only available on SME-published/produced books and videos. Discount is off the list price for a single title as follows: 20% off 11-25 items of the same title
25% off 26-50 items of the same title
30% off 51-99 items of the same title
40% off 100 or more items of the same title
All orders must be placed by telephone or by purchase order to receive a volume discount. This discount is not applicable with any other discount and cannot be applied to the SME member rate.

Shipping and Handling

Shipping and handling charges apply to all orders, except digital delivery (eBooks, video on-demand, etc.), unless otherwise indicated. Shipping and handling charges vary by the weight of the product(s) ordered and the destination of delivery. Online delivery options include FedEx ground, FedEx priority overnight, and FedEx international. Additional delivery options may be available by calling or emailing SME Customer Care.

Licensing

Licensing of SME-published book and video products is available in North America through Knowledge Edge. Call Tooling U-SME at 866.706.8665 for additional information. Licensing outside of North America for video streaming is available through Kanopy. Call Kanopy at 415.926.5293 or email sales@kanopystreaming.com.

International Orders

For information on authorized distributors, please call SME Customer Care at 313.425.3000 ext. 4500 between 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. ET, Monday-Friday. If ordering directly from SME, pre-payment in U.S. funds must accompany your order and checks must be drawn on a U.S. bank. We accept international American Express, MasterCard, Visa, and Discover. If payment does not accompany your order, a pro forma statement will be processed and mailed/faxed to you. When you return the pro forma with payment in U.S. funds, drawn on a U.S. bank, your order will be shipped promptly.
